Weather Tips

Useful tips for different weather conditions

Sunny Day Tips

  • Drink plenty of water
  • Wear sunglasses
  • Use sunscreen
  • Wear light clothes

Rainy Day Tips

  • Carry an umbrella
  • Wear waterproof shoes
  • Drive carefully
  • Avoid flooded areas

Windy Day Tips

  • Hold on to your hat
  • Close windows tightly
  • Drive slowly
  • Avoid tall trees

Foggy Day Tips

  • Drive with low beam
  • Maintain safe distance
  • Avoid over speeding
  • Use fog lights
